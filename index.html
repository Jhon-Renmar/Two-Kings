How we began

The journey of Two Kings' Cafe & Bar into the world of milktea began as a response to the growing demand for this beloved beverage among the local community.

Recognizing the trend and the opportunity it presented, the visionary owners of the café and bar decided to expand their menu offerings and introduce a delightful selection of milktea creations.
